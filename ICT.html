About Indian Cricket

The Indian cricket team, also known as Team India or the Men in Blue, is one of the strongest and most popular cricket teams in the world. Governed by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI), the team has won multiple ICC tournaments, including the 1983 and 2011 World Cups, the 2007 T20 World Cup, and the 2013 Champions Trophy. With legendary players like Sachin Tendulkar, MS Dhoni, and Virat Kohli, Rohit Sharma ,Jasprit Bumrah,and rising stars like Shubman Gill, Yashasvi Jaiswal, Shreyas Iyer, Hardik pandya, the team continues to inspire millions of fans worldwide.

Player Achievements

Virat Kohli

  • Fastest to 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, and 11,000 ODI runs
  • ICC Cricketer of the Year (2017, 2018)
  • Most centuries in run-chases in ODIs
  • First player to score 20,000 international runs in a decade
  • Most double centuries by an Indian in Test cricket

Rohit Sharma

  • Only player to score three double centuries in ODIs
  • Highest individual score in ODIs (264 runs)
  • Most centuries in a single World Cup (5 in 2019)
  • Most centuries in a single World Cup (5 in 2019)
  • Won five IPL titles as Mumbai Indians captain

MS Dhoni

  • Only captain to win all three ICC trophies (T20 World Cup, ODI World Cup, Champions Trophy)
  • Most stumpings by any wicket-keeper in international cricket
  • First wicket-keeper to complete 100 stumpings in ODIs
  • Most matches as captain in T20Is
  • Led India to No. 1 ranking in Test cricket for the first time

Sachin Tendulkar

  • Most runs in international cricket (34,357 runs)
  • Most centuries in international cricket (100 centuries)
  • Only player to score 100 international centuries
  • First player to score a double century in ODIs
  • Most Man of the Match awards in international cricket

Jasprit Bumrah

  • First Asian bowler to take 5 wickets in a Test innings in South Africa, England, and Australia
  • Fastest Indian pacer to reach 50 Test wickets
  • First Indian bowler to take a Test hat-trick in the West Indies
  • ICC ODI Player of the Year 2019
  • Number 1 ranked ODI bowler in 2018

Rising Stars

  • Shubman Gill: Youngest player to score a double century in ODIs
  • Yashasvi Jaiswal: Youngest player to score an IPL double century
  • Shreyas Iyer: First player to score a century and half-century on debut in both Test and ODI
  • Hardik Pandya: First Indian to take 4 wickets and score 30+ runs in a T20I
  • Rishabh Pant: Fastest Indian wicket-keeper to 1000 Test runs
